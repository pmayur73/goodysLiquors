About Us

Your Favorite
Neighborhood Store.

"We built this store for this neighborhood — and we take that personally."

Goody's Corks N' Hops has been the go-to bottle shop for Mansfield and the greater Storrs area. Conveniently located at 1019 Stafford Road, we're just minutes from the UConn campus and right at the heart of the community we call home.

We stock a carefully curated range of wines from every major region, an ever-changing craft beer selection, and a spirits aisle that balances everyday value with premium finds. Our staff genuinely loves what they do — and it shows.

Whether you're stocking up for game day, finding the right gift, or picking out something special for dinner, we're here to make it easy and enjoyable. Come visit us — we'd love to help.
